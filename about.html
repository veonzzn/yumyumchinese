Our Story

The Heart Behind Yum Yum Chinese

Yum Yum Chinese Restaurant is more than just a place to eat; it is the result of decades of passion, family dedication, and a deep love for authentic Chinese cuisine.

Chef Phillip Mah began his culinary journey at a young age, working alongside his two brothers, Ken Ma and Don Ma, at Janbo's on the west side of Evansville. Those early years in the kitchen shaped his skills, discipline, and commitment to delivering quality dishes rooted in tradition.

As Phillip and his wife Ying began building their own family, they made the decision to branch out and create something of their own. This led to the opening of Great Wall in Princeton, Kentucky, where they developed their signature style — combining authentic flavors with a welcoming, family-oriented dining experience.

With time, family became a greater priority. Phillip and Ying returned to Evansville to be closer to loved ones, valuing the importance of being present during life's most meaningful moments. After settling back in, their passion for serving the community led them to open Mandarin Garden, continuing their journey in the restaurant industry.

In 2018, Yum Yum Chinese Restaurant was born in Henderson, Kentucky. With years of experience behind them, Phillip and Ying set out to bring high-quality Chinese cuisine to Henderson and the surrounding communities — offering not just great food, but a place where customers feel like family.

Today, Yum Yum Chinese continues to be a reflection of their journey, built on hard work, resilience, and a genuine passion for sharing great food with the people they serve.
